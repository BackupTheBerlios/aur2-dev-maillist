The placement of the header is slightly confusing. Since the "Search  
Criteria" header is right-aligned, it looks like "Recent Updates" is  
the header of the page (it's also the same level, which shouldn't be  
the case). Removing "Recent Updates" and placing the RSS icon to the  
right would be better, but it would imply that the RSS feed is of the  
search, rather than recent updates. Like I said in our conversation  
there's no good place to put this at the moment. I'd probably put it  
somewhere in the footer, but we don't really have a footer. Using a  
browser's in-built RSS capabilities should be enough for now. It's not  
just a Mozilla Firefox feature. All browsers I know of support this,  
including Internet Explorer, Firefox, Opera, Chrome, Safari, etc.
